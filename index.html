Research (Motivation)

Real-world perception systems operate in constantly changing environments with unpredictable data shifts, highlighting the need to build systems that can generalize beyond training data. Developing these robust solutions is particularly important for applications like autonomous vehicles and healthcare, where obtaining sufficient labeled data is difficult and supervised learning methods fall short. Inspired by human cognition, I aim to develop AI systems that interpret complex environments with minimal supervision, adapting effectively to new contexts. Motivated by these challenges, I am interested to work in the areas of : Machine Learning, Computer Vision, and its application in Robotics.
